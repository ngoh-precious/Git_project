Play with Kubernetes

Play with Kubernet (PWK) is a web-based kubernetes playground that you can use for free. All you need is a web browser and an internet connection. It is faster, easiest way to get your hands on kubernetes.

Let's see what it looks like:

  1. Point your browser to http://play-with-k8s.com

  2. Confirm that you're a human and click + ADD NEW INSTANCE
